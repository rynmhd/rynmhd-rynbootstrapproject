Home & Private Parties

An amazing experience of 5000+ successful house parties, birthday party, cocktails dinners, 8 course sit-down and many more occasions catering with entire crockery, cutlery, bar-ware and service. Among all the other house party caterers in Mumbai, we are the only catering company which has catered to entire Bollywood industry for their home parties and birthday parties.

During the 20+ years in operation, we always strive to maintain good hygienic conditions and a clean environment both in and out of the food preparation zones. Ask our friendly team for advice on how we can create a party to remember!


Club Catering

We have catered many clubs in Mumbai, and the food & service were talk of town, from traditional dishes to Bohari thal, Kerala food, North Indian, Parsee dishes, pan Asian & European cuisines.


Destination Events

Make your event special with us, whether black tie or Island casual, GFC creates an unforgettable occasion with a perfectly prepared feast of delectable food served in a truly tropical setting.

Corporate Catering

Having many years of experience in corporate catering services in Mumbai, GFC has catered at a number of Mumbai’s top venues for every kind of corporate catering including conferences, product launches, fashion shows, charity events, barbeques, shop launch, office celebrations, working lunch, Hi-tea, summer and christmas parties.

Our creativity, culinary experience, event planning expertise and attention to detail has made hundreds of corporate and business clients satisfied and happy, and coming back again and again.

Wedding Catering

We are here to make your perfect wedding day come true!

When you choose Good Food Concept to cater for your wedding, you can be certain of many things - the food will look wonderful and taste delicious, the staff will be helpful and beautifully turned out and the tables will look stunning.

GFC is the listed wedding caterers in Mumbai at a number of glamorous Mumbai wedding venues and can advise on, which will be most suitable for your big day. Be it engagement, bridal showers, mehndi, sangeet or reception, our bespoke package will make your guests go “WOW”.


Banquet Halls & Venues

Good Food Concept offers a wide range of stunning banquet halls in Mumbai for all occasions; wedding, corporate event, party venue in Mumbai or a family reunion.
